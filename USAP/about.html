Meet Our Team

Two highschool students working for the better future of the immigrant community

Kristen Duron

I'm Kristen Duron, the heart behind this project. My inspiration came from a deeply personal experience when I discovered that some of my own family members were resorting to illegal means due to corruption in pursuit of success. In the midst of this, I also witnessed the dreams of many individuals who longed to come to the U.S. legally but didn't know how. Surrounded by stories of resilience and legal success, exemplified by my parents and my project partner, I was inspired to create this website. I aim to instill in the minds of those dreaming of coming to the USA that it is indeed possible.

Jonathan Falero

I’m Jonathan Falero, creating the link from our project to the community. As an immigrant myself, I have faced the lack of guidance many are encountered whether they want to come to America or once they’re here wondering what the next steps are. I created a website with my Computer Science background in hopes to serve the people with simplified information, mantaining in mind my prupose of helping out.

Together we thrive

Our mission is simple: to guide aspiring individuals like my family members towards legal pathways, illuminating the way to the opportunities they deserve.This website stands as a testament to the achievable path toward realizing your dreams.
